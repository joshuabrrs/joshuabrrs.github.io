Anime Mood

iOS app made in SwampHacks

Anime Mood is an iOS app that asks you for your mood and depending on your mood it returns a list of Anime that would either reinfornce your mood or make you feel better. This app was created at SwampHacks '17 along with a friend. It is native so we used Swift and XCode to build it. S.I.T.E

Web App for Y-Combinator Course.

S.I.T.E stands for Student Information for Teachers Electronically. A team of 3 developers and I are taking a Y Combinator course where we are given tips for creating a startup business. With our web app we intend to replace a terminal-like system administrators have to use and type in codes in order to take attendance and view student records. We want to make it a lot more modern and stress-free for administrators and teachers. The current system they use is built off technology from the 1990s and it is vunerable to attacks as well. We are still in development are will update more information along the way.
